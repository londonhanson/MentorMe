About Us

MentorMe is an educational enterprise program designed to connect employees within an organization. Mentorship programs often run into problems, such as:

  • Pairing mentors with mentees
  • Identifying mentors that want to teach
  • Providing engaging content to teach mentees
Through our dynamic application, mentors are able to create classes of their own expertise by uploading resources conveniently and accessibly. Mentees can then choose lessons based on the skills they need to learn.

Prepare your employees in an engaging way with MentorMe today!
